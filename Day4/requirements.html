Presentation Requirements

Make sure your team is prepared and ready to shine!

Checklist for Your Presentation:

  • Each team should use Google Slides for their presentation
  • Each team member should present
  • Presentation should be around 15 minutes (10 minutes for talk, 5 for Q&A)
  • Introduce your team and describe the problem you are trying to solve
  • Discuss the dataset you are working with
  • Explain your approach or methodology
  • Include visuals, demos, or slides if possible
  • Talk about the future plans or future works
  • Conclude with what you learned or built as a team
  • Speak clearly and respectfully, show confidence and creativity!
